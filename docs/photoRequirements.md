# Photo Requirements Document

## Overview
This document defines all image requirements for the Purple Bags website to ensure visual consistency, optimal performance, and premium presentation. All images must be compressed and served in WebP format.

## Hero Images
- **Subject**: High-end lifestyle photography of premium wedding bags in wedding settings.
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 (Desktop) / 3:4 or 9:16 (Mobile).
- **Resolution**: Min 1920x1080px (Desktop), Min 1080x1920px (Mobile).
- **Style**: Soft lighting, elegant backgrounds, focus on material quality.

## Factory / Manufacturing Images
- **Subject**: Clean, organized factory setting showing the manufacturing process, stitching, and printing.
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 or 3:2.
- **Resolution**: Min 1200x800px.
- **Style**: Well-lit, professional, highlighting craftsmanship and scale of operations.

## Collection Images
- **Subject**: Individual bags or sets representing specific collections (Jute, Cotton, etc.).
- **Aspect Ratio**: 1:1 or 4:5.
- **Resolution**: Min 1080x1080px.
- **Style**: Consistent studio lighting, isolated on solid or softly textured background (e.g., lavender or light gray), showing depth and texture.

## Gallery Images
- **Subject**: Past projects, customized bags from real weddings.
- **Aspect Ratio**: Mixed (portrait and landscape) for Masonry layout.
- **Resolution**: Min 1080px width or height.
- **Style**: Authentic, high-quality, showing custom prints clearly.

## Printing Process Images
- **Subject**: Close-ups of Screen Printing, Offset Printing, and DTF Printing techniques.
- **Aspect Ratio**: 3:2 or 16:9.
- **Resolution**: Min 1200x800px.
- **Style**: Macro photography showing ink texture, clarity of print, and quality.

## Material Images
- **Subject**: Close-ups of Jute, Cotton, Fabric, Non-Woven textures.
- **Aspect Ratio**: 1:1.
- **Resolution**: Min 800x800px.
- **Style**: High-contrast macro shots emphasizing weave and thread quality.

## Technical Guidelines
- Maximum file size: <250KB per image (after WebP compression).
- File naming convention: lowercase-with-hyphens.webp (e.g., `jute-wedding-bag-premium.webp`).
- Alt text is mandatory for all images.
